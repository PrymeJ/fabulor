## 1. `self.player.chapter` for display/navigation — (a) present & correct

`.chapter` appears at three sites in [player.py](src/fabulor/player.py), none used for the prohibited "map a playback position to a chapter for UI display":

- [player.py:607-611](src/fabulor/player.py#L607-L611) — the `chapter` **property getter** itself. For VT it walks `_chapter_list` (the sanctioned algorithm); for non-VT it returns `self.instance.chapter`. This is the definition, not a forbidden consumer.
- [player.py:615](src/fabulor/player.py#L615) — `self.instance.chapter = value` inside the **setter** (see #2).
- [player.py:841](src/fabulor/player.py#L841) — `curr_idx = self.chapter` in `apply_smart_rewind`, used only to look up a chapter *start time* as a rewind floor, not to drive the displayed chapter. Acceptable.

The UI-facing chapter derivation lives in `_on_time_pos_change` ([player.py:110](src/fabulor/player.py#L110)) and `_on_file_loaded_populate_chapters` ([app.py:1196](src/fabulor/app.py#L1196)), both of which walk `chapter_list` with the `+ _CHAPTER_BOUNDARY_EPSILON` (0.35) tolerance. Correct.
